Peeking inside a tiny GPT

This demo trains a single-block, character-level GPT in the browser using TensorFlow.js. The corpus is the Shakespeare sonnets dataset popularized by nanoGPT. Use the buttons below to load the text, train the model, and inspect the learned attention patterns.

Hyperparameters

Sample the model

Provide a prompt, choose how many new characters to generate, and optionally adjust the sampling temperature. Lower temperatures make the output more deterministic.
